Support

We're here to help! If you're experiencing issues with any ZionX app, please review the common solutions below or contact us directly.

Subscription & Billing

  • Cancel subscription: Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions on your iPhone/iPad
  • Restore purchases: Open the app > Settings > Restore Purchases
  • Billing questions: All billing is handled by Apple. Visit support.apple.com/billing

App Issues

  • App crashes: Ensure you're running the latest version from the App Store
  • Data lost: Try restarting the app. Data is stored locally on your device
  • Feature not working: Check that you have an active subscription in Settings
